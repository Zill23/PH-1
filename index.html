Bangladesh 2.0

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation refers to the process of creating and implementing new ideas, products, or services that bring about significant improvements or transformations. It involves thinking creatively and embracing change to solve problems, meet needs, or seize opportunities. Innovation can occur in various fields, including technology, business, healthcare, and education, and it often leads to increased efficiency, productivity, and competitiveness

Sustainability

Sustainability is about meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. It involves a balanced approach to economic growth, environmental protection, and social well-being. In essence, sustainability aims to create a healthy, equitable, and resilient world for both current and future generations.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the fundamental physical systems and structures needed for the operation of a society or enterprise. This includes transportation networks (like roads, bridges, and railways), utilities (such as water supply, sewage, and electricity), telecommunications, and public services (including schools, hospitals, and government buildings)

Empowerment

Empowerment refers to the process of enabling individuals or groups to gain control over their own lives, make their own choices, and achieve their potential. It involves providing the tools, resources, education, and opportunities needed to build confidence, self-reliance, and a sense of ownership over one’s actions and decisions.
